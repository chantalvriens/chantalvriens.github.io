Welcome

Hey there! Welcome, my name is Chantal Vriens and I proudly present to you on this page my designer development portfolio. Throughout this portfolio I will illustrate my growth as a designer during my Master Industrial Design at the Technical University of Eindhoven from September 2020 until July 2022. If you are reading this as someone unfamiliar to ID at the TU/e - the purpose of this portfolio is not simply to showcase my best work. Instead the focus will be on highlighting the progress to get where I am as a designer today and where I want to be in the future. To do this, I will first introduce you to my identity and vision as a designer, continue with an overview of all the projects I performed and ending with a reflective statement. For each these development shaping activities some important information will be highlighted on the right and further explanation will be provided on the left. If you are interested in even more in-depth information, the available links can be used to download the final deliverables, such as reports and reflections.

I hope you enjoy! Sincerely, Chantal
